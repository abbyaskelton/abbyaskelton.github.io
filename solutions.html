Solutions to Global Warming

Quick Solutions

Ride your bicycle to work or school instead of driving your car.

Long-Term Solutions

  • Plant more forests and repair damaged forests

  • Pass legislation that regulates transportation, enforces renewable energy, and regulates carbon emissions from large corporations

  • Fund carbon capture (storing carbon emissions underground so they cannot enter the atmosphere)

  • Use different, more Earth-friendly feed for livestock

Agriculture: The Most Overlooked Polluter

"High yield and large scale farming regulations haven't changed much in the last few decades and as a result the farming industry has run rampant with the pollution and damage that it causes to the environment. This includes ammonia pollution from animal waste, soil and water pollution from fertilizers, decreased biodiversity from harmful pesticides, and carbon emissions from transportation and farming vehicles."
